How does the internship process work?

Candidates apply through the careers portal, interact with mentors, and work on real-world projects with guided learning and innovation-focused tasks.

Is mentorship and guidance provided?

Yes. Every candidate receives mentorship, project guidance, technical support, and career-focused learning throughout the program.

Are internships remote or onsite?

Arshith Groups offers both remote and onsite opportunities depending on the project, department, and candidate preference.

What technologies are used?

Candidates work with modern technologies including React, Node.js, MongoDB, UI/UX tools, cloud platforms, and AI-driven solutions.
